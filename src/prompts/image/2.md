## 一、基础漫画风格

**适用场景：** 将真实照片或描述转换为清新、活泼的动漫风格。

### 完整提示词

```
(masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed, high resolution, 8K),
anime style, illustration,
beautiful and expressive character, vibrant colors, soft shading,
frontal view, looking directly at the camera, symmetrical face,
natural and confident expression,
detailed anime face, big sparkling eyes, small nose, soft lips, perfect skin,
smooth shading,
detailed hair with dynamic flow, bright and lively,
cinematic lighting, bokeh effect, clean and detailed background.
```

### 核心关键词解析

| 关键词 | 说明 |
|--------|------|
| `masterpiece, best quality` | 提升整体画质 |
| `frontal view` | 正面视角，便于人物清晰呈现 |
| `big sparkling eyes` | 动漫标志性大眼睛 |
| `soft shading` | 柔和渐变的阴影效果 |
| `bokeh effect` | 背景虚化，突出主体 |
